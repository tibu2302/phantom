# Bybit SP500 Trading Research

## Findings:
1. **SP500USDT** — Perpetual contract on Bybit that tracks S&P 500 index. USDT-settled. Available at https://www.bybit.com/trade/usdt/SP500USDT
2. **SPX500MUSDT** — Another SP500 perpetual contract (mini version). Available at https://www.bybit.com/trade/usdt/SPX500MUSDT
3. **SPXUSDT** — This is SPX6900 (a meme coin), NOT the S&P 500 index
4. **TradFi Perpetual Contracts** — Bybit offers USDT-denominated perpetual contracts that track traditional financial instruments (indices, forex, commodities)
5. These follow the same margin, funding rate, and liquidation mechanisms as standard USDT perpetual contracts
6. Can go LONG and SHORT on SP500USDT
7. Category is "linear" (USDT-settled)

## Key: SP500USDT is the correct symbol for S&P 500 index on Bybit linear perpetuals
